# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action.
